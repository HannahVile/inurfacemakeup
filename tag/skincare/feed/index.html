Welcome to the first in a series of reviews that are completely not sponsored & brutally honest.

First up is Augustinus Bader

The Cream and The Rich Cream

One of the reasons I wanted to do this review first is because this is one of my top two favorite brands. Everything you’ve heard about Augustinus Bader is true, this is the sh*t when it comes to anti-aging skincare!

Quick run down,  this was made by a globally recognized leader in stem cell research and regenerative medicine, long story short Augie knows his stuff! It took 30 yrs of research to make this. In 2008 he developed a groundbreaking Wound Gel that heals severe skin traumas without the need for surgery or skin grafts. ( yadda yadda yadda, here’s all you need to know) With a focus on the reawakening of cells that become dormant due to aging or trauma, It triggers your skin into renewing itself like it did when it was younger. Remember, this dude is the best in the biz when it comes to regenerative medicine.

Is that who I want taking care of my skincare needs? Ummm yeahhh Absolutely!

•it’s a “one – and – done” product . Meaning; it’s not JUST your moisturizer, it’s your serum, your eye cream & your neck cream.

•Aaaaand it works!

There’s two formulations.

The Cream & The Rich Cream.
price $170 30ml.   $265 50ml.

I use the Cream during the day ( because it’s lighter and has more slip, so  my makeup goes on better with it). I use The Rich Cream at night, because even though I’m slightly oily (thank you mom) I like the whole “slather on heavier cream at night” routine.

This one does not disappoint, they do a 27 day challenge at Augustinus Bader, and the results will blow your mind!

(This is not me, this is one of the company’s before and after results)

 

 

As far as my review goes I give this a 10 out of 10!
